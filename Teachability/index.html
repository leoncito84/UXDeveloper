faq

Neurodiversity refers to the natural variation in human brain function and cognition, including conditions like autism, ADHD, dyslexia, and others. It emphasizes that these differences should be recognized and valued as part of human diversity, rather than being viewed as deficits. Our platform celebrates neurodiversity by offering resources and support tailored to individual learning styles and needs.

To find the right course, start by exploring our course catalog, which is organized by learning needs and specialties. You can use filters to select courses that align with your specific interests or challenges. Additionally, our platform offers personalized recommendations based on your profile and learning goals. If you need further assistance, our support team is available to help guide you in choosing the best course.

We offer a range of support options, including personalized learning resources, one-on-one mentoring, and interactive workshops. Our platform also provides access to a community forum where students can connect and share experiences. Additionally, we offer tools and strategies tailored to various neurodiverse conditions to help enhance your learning experience and academic success.

To request accommodations, you can submit a request through our platform's support section. Provide details about the accommodations you need and any relevant documentation. Our team will review your request and work with you to implement the necessary adjustments to your learning environment or course materials. If you have any questions or need assistance with the process, our support staff is here to help.

To connect with a mentor, browse our mentor directory and review profiles to find someone whose expertise matches your needs. You can then use the "Contact" or "Schedule a Meeting" button on their profile to arrange an initial consultation. If you need assistance, our support team is available to help you with the scheduling process and to answer any questions you might have.
